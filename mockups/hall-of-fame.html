A Legacy Carved in Stone

The OpenDominion Hall of Fame exists to celebrate the individuals whose extraordinary contributions shaped not just a game, but a community. From the very first line of code to the fiercest battles waged across countless rounds, these inductees represent the heart and soul of OpenDominion. Their dedication — whether through building, teaching, or competing — has left an indelible mark on every player who has ever raised a realm.

The Inaugural Class

2026 Inductees

W

WaveHack

The Architect

The founder and lead developer of OpenDominion. WaveHack created the open-source recreation of the classic Dominion browser game from scratch, building the entire codebase and infrastructure. He devoted years of work to keeping the spirit of Dominion alive for a new generation of players. His vision and tireless technical dedication made everything possible.

D

Dreki

The Strategist

A legendary strategist and community pillar. Known for deep game knowledge, mentoring newer players, and helping shape game balance through thoughtful feedback and testing. Dreki's contributions to strategy guides and community discussions elevated the entire player base's understanding of the game and set the standard for competitive play.

B

Blackreign

The Conqueror

A dominant force on the battlefield and a passionate community advocate. Known for aggressive military tactics and memorable round performances, Blackreign brought intensity and excitement to every round. His contributions to community building and player recruitment helped OpenDominion grow into the thriving community it is today.
