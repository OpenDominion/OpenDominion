Monarchy

From The Dominion Encyclopedia
Jump to: navigation, search

The monarch of a realm is elected by the dominions in the realm. It is the monarch who handles the realm's diplomatic relations. In ages past, the monarch received a +10% platinum production bonus but that has been removed as it was abused and caused fights in less organized and united realms. Monarchs can moderate the council, meaning that they can delete anyone's posts. It is the monarch who sets the realm's realm name and the login news page. He/she is also the only person in the realm that can check for any inactivity of his realm mates.

Role

The role of a monarch is subjective but generally is viewed to be of someone with decent experience in dominion. Thus, he/she may be the one who gives advices to newbies and answers questions in the council. The monarch also makes the final decision with regards to diplomacy (war, black ops or wonders) although he may not be the first person to initiate the plan. Some may also argue that it is the monarchs' role to act as a leader of the realm by being active and tries to help everyone in the realm, as well as to provide ops support to keep track of safe DP and even top OP for attackers.

King or Queen

Dominions with a male ruler name is designated King, whereas dominions with a female ruler name is designated Queen.

Baron

Barons are exclusive to Dominion Liege Lord. The monarch of the alliance appoints barons (or baronesses), who can moderate the council and send out invitations.
